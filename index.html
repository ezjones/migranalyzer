Migranalyzer

Migraines analyzer

Migraines can be caused by weather changes.

But there is not much info about how, why or when they happen. Some people believe it's the Barometric Pressure that causes headaches, others think it all a myth. I have tried all kinds of medicines and alternative medicine solutions but there is nothing that really works. There is also "Migraine Predictors" out there but I found that they don't work at all. The project is in the initial stages. Now I am capturing the weather and my "Headache amount" and trying to compare the two. In the future I will introduce more variables to compare. Below you can see realtime charts of the weather and a "Headache amount" chart.

How it works:

I have a python script running on Azure on a Linux Vm. From there I grab the Weather Forecast and send it to Google SpreadSheets. There I create and publish these charts. The Headache Amount is the only data entered manually by me. I try to enter it manually several times a day. Eventually I will create the charts in python too and skig Gdocs but for now it works.
